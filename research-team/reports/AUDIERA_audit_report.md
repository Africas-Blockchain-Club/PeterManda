## Phase 1.5: Tokenomics & AMM Math

> **Why we look at this:** Most DEX tokens trade inside Automated Market Makers (AMMs - a type of decentralised exchange that uses a mathematical formula to price assets instead of an order book). The AMM formula directly determines how much price moves when someone buys or sells. This section runs the actual maths so you know exactly what happens to price when capital enters or exits.

### 1.5.1 Liquidity Pool Equation

The most common AMM uses the Constant Product formula: x multiplied by y equals k. In plain English: the pool holds two assets, and the rule is that multiplying their quantities together must always equal the same fixed number (k). When a buyer takes base tokens (BEAT) out of the pool, the pool has fewer BEAT tokens remaining. To keep the product equal to k, the price of each remaining BEAT token must rise. The fewer BEAT tokens left in the pool, the higher the price. This is why large buys cause price spikes and large sells cause price crashes - especially in thin pools where the total quantities are small relative to the trade size.

Now, examining the AUDIERA Uniswap pool data from DexScreener:

| Variable | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Base Token Qty in pool (BEAT) | 499,999,998 BEAT |
| Quote Token Qty in pool (ETH/WETH equivalent) | 0.00009836 ETH |
| Constant Product k = Base x Quote | 499,999,998 x 0.00009836 = 49,179.99 |
| Current Price (implied) | $0.1559 per BEAT |
| Estimated price after 10% base removed | k / (499,999,998 x 0.90) = 49,179.99 / 449,999,998.2 = 0.0000001093 ETH per BEAT |
| Estimated price impact of 10% removal | Approximately +11.1% price increase on the quoted asset side |

**Calculation shown explicitly:**
- k = 499,999,998 x 0.00009836 = 49,179.99
- New Base Qty after 10% removed = 499,999,998 x 0.90 = 449,999,998.2
- New implied price = 49,179.99 / 449,999,998.2 = 0.0000001093 ETH per BEAT
- Original implied price = 49,179.99 / 499,999,998 = 0.00000009836 ETH per BEAT
- Price impact = (0.0000001093 - 0.00000009836) / 0.00000009836 = approximately +11.1%

> **Reading this table:** A 10% removal of base tokens from this specific pool produces an estimated +11.1% price impact on the formula side. However, the more critical anomaly here is the quote token quantity: 0.00009836 ETH is worth approximately $0.30 USD at current ETH prices. This means the quote side of the Uniswap pool contains essentially no stablecoin or ETH liquidity. The reported $77.98 million (ZAR 1.48 billion) in total DEX liquidity appears to be almost entirely denominated in BEAT tokens, not in the counter-asset. This structure is characteristic of a pool where the team has deposited their own tokens to create the appearance of liquidity depth without committing meaningful counter-asset capital.
