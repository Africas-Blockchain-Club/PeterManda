### **The Blueprint – 11 Steps (do in order)**

**Step 0: Token Classification**

Answer these questions first:

* Native coin of its own L1/chain? (BTC, ETH, SOL, HYPE, TRX, ADA → highest utility)  
* Token on another chain? (ERC-20, SPL, BEP-20 → gas paid in base asset)  
* Type: governance only / utility / revenue-share / memecoin (fair launch or pre-mine) / security token?  
* Regulatory risk level? (high for revenue-share or security-like tokens)

**Step 1: Official Narrative**  
Go to project website → “tokenomics”, “token”, “docs”, “allocation”, “whitepaper”.  
Screenshot the pie chart, claimed supply, utility, burns, etc.

(Note: this is marketing — verify everything later.)

**Step 2: Professional Rating (optional but smart)**  
Visit [https://audit.tokenomics.com/](https://audit.tokenomics.com/) → search the token.

Note the letter grade (AAA–B) and any red flags they highlight.

**Step 3: Explorer \= Source of Truth**  
Open the correct explorer above.

Record:

* Max supply  
* Total supply  
* Circulating supply  
* Top 10–20 wallets (labels \= team, treasury, burn, airdrop, staking)  
* Recent large transactions (unlocks, burns, team moves)  
* For tokens: read contract → totalSupply() function  
* Burn address: check cumulative tokens sent there

**Step 4: Supply Model & Emission/Deflation**  
Fixed max cap?  
Inflation schedule or halving?  
Burn mechanisms? (visible on explorer)

Disinflationary or perpetual inflation?

**Step 5: Allocation \+ Vesting/Unlocks (dilution calendar)**

* Official pie chart (from Step 1\)  
* DefiLlama Unlocks page (best visual schedule)  
* Tokenomist.ai (cross-check)  
* Manual proof: watch team/treasury wallets on explorer (outflows \= real unlocks)

**Step 6: Utility & Demand Drivers**

What does the token **actually** do?

* Gas token?  
* Required for staking/validators?  
* Fee discounts?  
* Governance voting?  
* Revenue share?

**Step 7: Revenue Capture (the value engine)**  
DefiLlama → search protocol or chain → “Fees & Revenue” tab.

Key question: What % of fees/revenue actually flows to token holders (buybacks, burns, staking rewards, dividends)?

**Step 8: Community & Adoption Health (beyond hype)**

On-chain signals (strongest proof):

* Daily active addresses & unique wallets (Dune or chain dashboard)  
* Transaction count & holder distribution  
* % of supply in top 10 wallets vs widely held

Social & governance signals:

* Real X/Discord engagement (not just price spam)  
* GitHub activity (commits, contributors)  
* DAO vote participation rate  
* Long-term holders (check explorer labels)

**Step 9: Governance & Incentives**  
Staking APY?  
Voting power?

Proposal process (Snapshot or on-chain)?

**Step 10: Red Flags & Final Verdict**

Common red flags:

* Team/investors \>30 % with \<2-year vesting  
* High perpetual inflation \+ no revenue to holders  
* Anonymous team \+ weak on-chain community  
* All supply “circulating” but top wallets hold 60–80 %

Your verdict: 1–10 score \+ 1-sentence summary.

**Step 11: Monitoring Setup**

Bookmark:

* Explorer token page  
* DefiLlama unlocks \+ fees pages  
* audit.tokenomics.com page  
* 2–3 key wallets (team, burn, treasury) Set monthly reminder or alerts on large wallet movements.
